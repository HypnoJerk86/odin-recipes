Lasagna

Lasagna is easy dish to make, delicious and full of flavor. A hearty Italian meal that will be enjoyed by everyone in the family.

Below you will find a list of ingredients required for this dish.

Ingredients

  • 300g lasagne sheets
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 200ml beef stock
  • Passata sauce
  • Cheese sauce
  • 125g mozzarella
  • Olive oil
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over
  2. Pour over 800g passata and add 200ml beef stock. Add grated nutmeg to season.
  3. Bring to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich.
  4. Heat oven to 180C/fan/160C/gas mark 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish.
  5. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagna sheet from a 300g pack. Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-made or homemade cheese sauce.
  6. Repeat untill you have 3 layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g cheese sauce, making sure you can't see any pasta.
  7. Scatter 125g torn mozzarella over the top.
  8. Bake for 45 mins untill the top is bubbling and lightly browned.
