Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed peppers are simple to make and are delicious, a great alernative for a friday night dinner.

Below you will find a list of ingredients required for this dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Medium onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 450g ground beef
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • black pepper
  • 6 Bell peppers
  • Monterey jack cheese
  • Freshly chopped parsly to garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. In a small saucepan, prepare rice according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Cook onion until soft, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute more. Add ground beef and cook until no longer pink, 6 minutes. Drain fat.
  4. Return beef mixture to skillet, add diced tomatoes. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Let simmer until liquid has reduced slightly, about 5 minutes.
  5. Place peppers cut side-up in a 9"-x-13" baking dish and drizzle with oil.
  6. Spoon beef mixture into each pepper and top with Monterey jack, then cover baking dish with foil.
  7. Bake until peppers are tender, about 35 minutes. Uncover and bake until cheese is bubbly, 10 minutes more.
  8. Garnish with parsley before serving.
  9. Enjoy!
