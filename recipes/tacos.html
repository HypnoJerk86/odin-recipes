Tacos

Tacos are simple to make and are delicious, a great alernative for a friday night dinner.

Below you will find a list of ingredients required for this dish.

Ingredients

  • 450g lean beef mince
  • A jar of chunky salsa
  • 10 taco shells
  • Half a head of lettuce (shredded)
  • Medium sized tomato
  • 200g shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef over medium heat for 10 mins, stirring occasionally, untill brown; drain.
  2. Stir salsa into beef, Heat to boiling, stirring constantly; reduce heat to medium-low. Cook 5 mins, stirring occasionally. Pour beef mixture into a serving bowl.
  3. Heat taco shells as directed on the packaging. Serve taco shells with beef mixture, lettuce, tomato and cheese.
  4. Enjoy!
